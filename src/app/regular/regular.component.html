HOW HAPPY MILES WORKS?

Drive yourself to an adventure and back in 5 simple steps

BOOK

Search for a car based on our services and book a car on our website with reasonalble prices

UPLOAD LICENSE

Upload your driver’s license for small security reasons, and pay a small security deposit.

UNLOCK

We SMS your car details 20 minutes before pickup.Unlock it via the Zoomcar app.

HAPPY

Fill the start checklist in the HappyMiles app. Grab the keys from the glove-box and drive.

RETURN

Return the car to the same location and fill the end checklist to end your trip..Happy Journey to all!!
